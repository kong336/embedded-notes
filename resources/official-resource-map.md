## Recommended Learning Order

1. Arduino or block-based tooling for fast starts
2. ESP-IDF or STM32Cube for deeper embedded work
3. PlatformIO for project management
4. FreeRTOS after basic peripheral work is stable
5. KiCad when moving from modules to board-level design
